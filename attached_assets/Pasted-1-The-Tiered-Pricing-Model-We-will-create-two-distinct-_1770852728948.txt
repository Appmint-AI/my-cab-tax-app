1. The Tiered Pricing Model
We will create two distinct plans in the Stripe Dashboard to manage this:

Basic (Free): Manual entry only. Drivers type in their own gross income and fees.

Pro (Paid): Unlocks the "Smart Sales Importer" (Auto-Grossing) and the 7-year Tax Vault.

Tell the Replit Agent:

"In the user database, add a subscription_tier field (default: 'basic'). Create a middleware function checkProAccess that guards the /api/income/auto-gross endpoint. If a user is not 'pro', return a 403 error with a message: 'Upgrade to Pro to unlock Auto-Grossing for Uber and Lyft.'"

2. Implementing the "Auto-Grossing" Logic
Since you want to charge for this, the UI should act as an "Upsell" the moment they try to use it.

The Logic for the Replit Agent:

"Build the Income Entry screen with two tabs: 'Manual' and 'Auto-Import (Pro)'.

If 'Auto-Import' is clicked by a Basic user, show a 'locked' state with a button: 'Upgrade to Pro for Automatic 1099-K Matching'.

For Pro users, implement the 25% Gross-Up Math:

Input: Net Payout (e.g., $750).

Calculated Gross: Net / 0.75 ($1,000).

Calculated Fee: Gross - Net ($250).

Save these as two separate records: one as Income and one as Expense (Platform Fees)."

3. Stripe Integration (The "Paywall")
To make the feature actually generate revenue, we need the "Upgrade" flow.

The Instruction for the Replit Agent:

"Create a /api/stripe/create-checkout-session endpoint. When a user clicks 'Upgrade', redirect them to a Stripe Checkout page for the Pro Plan.

Once payment is successful, use a Stripe Webhook (checkout.session.completed) to update the user's subscription_tier to 'pro' in our database.

Once the tier is updated, immediately reveal the Auto-Grossing feature in the UI."

4. The "Value Pitch" (Why they will pay extra)
In the USA, drivers are terrified of the IRS. We sell this feature as "Audit Insurance."

Marketing Copy for the Upgrade Modal:

Stop Guessing Your 1099-K. "Uber and Lyft report your 'Gross Fares' to the IRS, but only pay you the 'Net.' If these don't match on your tax return, you risk an audit.

Unlock Pro to:

Automatically Gross-Up: We do the math to ensure your records match your 1099-K perfectly.

Auto-Deduct Fees: Instantly categorize platform commissions to maximize your deductions.
