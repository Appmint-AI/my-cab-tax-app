Here is how we build the submission/export module to be "IRS-Ready."

1. The IRS "Category Mapping"
We shouldn't just let drivers type in random categories. We need to force their expenses into the specific buckets the IRS uses. This makes their tax filing seamless and reduces errors.

Tell the Replit Agent:

"Create a 'Submissions' data model. Every expense must be categorized into one of these IRS-standard buckets:

Car and Truck Expenses (Gas, oil, repairs, insurance)

Commissions and Fees (Uber/Lyft/Dispatch fees)

Insurance (Business-specific)

Interest (Car loan interest)

Legal and Professional Services (Accounting/License fees)

Office Expense (Cell phone data/Supplies)

Other Expenses (Water for passengers, car washes)"

2. The "Mileage Log" (The #1 Audit Item)
The IRS is strictest about mileage. A simple "total" isn't enough; they require a contemporaneous log.

Tell the Replit Agent:

"Build a Mileage Tracker module. For every entry, the driver must provide:

Date

Business Purpose (e.g., 'Shift Start to Shift End' or 'Fare to Airport')

Start Odometer (Optional but recommended)

End Odometer (Optional but recommended)

Total Miles Logic: Include a toggle for 'Standard Mileage Rate' (e.g., 67 cents per mile for 2024/2025) vs. 'Actual Expenses'."

3. The "Submission Preview" Screen
Before they export, they need to see a "Tax Summary" that looks like a draft of their tax return.

Tell the Replit Agent:

"Create a 'Tax Summary' dashboard view. It should aggregate all data into a 'Year-to-Date' summary.

Total Gross Income

Total Deductions (Sum of all categories)

Net Business Income/Loss (Gross - Deductions)

Estimated Tax Liability (Simple calculation: Net Income * 15.3% for Self-Employment tax)"

4. The "Export for IRS" Logic
Since we aren't "filing" yet, we are providing the Evidence Pack.

Tell the Replit Agent:

"Create an 'Export' function that generates a ZIP file containing:

A CSV Summary mapped to Schedule C categories.

A Detailed Mileage Log in PDF format.

A Receipt Folder containing all images uploaded for that tax year. Crucial: Add the legal watermark to the PDF: 'Self-Generated Record - Not Audited'."
